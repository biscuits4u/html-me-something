Background

I've played games since I was a little guy, on the computer and consoles. I found Twitch in about 2012 through Starcraft tournaments. I used to play Starcraft a lot as a kid and I really enjoyed watching high level professional play. I was a viewer on Twitch for years before I decided in 2016 to go ahead and set up my own stream to broadcast my own gameplay. I purchased the minimum amount of equipment necessary and got started in October 2016. I played all different kinds of games and continue to do so today. We have a great community of people who enjoy watching me struggle at playing video games.

Schedule

My schedule currently is all over the place. My day job and Launch Code take up the majority of my time. I try to stream twice a week. I tweet out whenever I'm going live.

I would love to make a career out of streaming on Twitch but the field is very saturated. It's not impossible but it will take a lot of time (a decent bit of luck) and hard work.
